About Niyantri Cafe

Niyantri Cafe is built on a simple idea: great coffee, thoughtful hospitality, and a warm, welcoming space where conversations flow as freely as the coffee. We source beans from small-batch roasters and roast to bring out the nuanced flavors of each origin.

From our cozy seating to the soft lighting and curated playlist, every detail is designed to help you slow down and savor the moment. We believe coffee should be an experience, not just a drink.

Warm Ambiance

A cozy, inviting space with wooden accents and soft textures.

Crafted Cocktails

Specialty drinks that pair beautifully with our pastries.

Community Events

Book clubs, coffee tastings, and open mic nights a few times a month.
